PunchBuddy

How to record
your voice-over

Follow these steps — you don't need any Pro Tools knowledge.

The Workflow
1

Import a Sequence

Open Interplay Access and select the sequence you want there. Then press the Import button on the Stream Deck.

Stream Deck → Import

PunchBuddy loads the sequence into Pro Tools automatically. Wait until the progress bar disappears.

2

Set the Microphone Level (Auto Gain)

Press the Auto Gain button on the Stream Deck (or in the PunchBuddy menu "Vocaster → Autogain Host"). PunchBuddy starts the automatic level setting right away — you don't need to press anything on the device.

Stream Deck → Auto Gain

A window appears saying "The automatic microphone level setting has started". Now speak into the microphone for about 10 seconds at your voice-over volume. The window briefly reports the result and closes on its own — done.

3

Record the Voice-Over

Put on the headphones. Press the Record Spr button on the Stream Deck — you'll hear a short pre-roll, then recording starts. Speak your text.

Stream Deck → Record Spr

Press Play/Stop to stop.
During recording, the mix runs automatically in the background.

4

Export the Finished Result

Once the recording is good, press Export. PunchBuddy trims, normalizes and delivers everything to Interplay NEXIS automatically.

Stream Deck → Export

Please do not switch to other programs during the export. The progress bar shows the status.

Make sure the finished sequence is in the correct folder of the Central Transfer. The finished sequence starts with "sf" for broadcast version (Sendefassung). Notify your colleague via intercom or phone (4590) for an immediate transfer to the playout server.
